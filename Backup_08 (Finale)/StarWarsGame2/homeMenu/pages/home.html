About The Game


Thunder Warrior: Genesis is a game that takes place in the Warstar universe where the Empire and the Rebellion battle for control of the galaxy. It follows the story of a Thunder Warrior, a soldier who uses powerful equipment, weapons, and special abilities to crush their foes. Choose your side: defend the Empire from the Rebellion, or desert the Empire and join the Rebellion to take the Empire down. Thunder Warrior: Genesis is a 3D third person shooter that uses High Definition 3D graphics to give the best possible gaming experience. Sign up, customize your character, and jump into the action! As you continue playing, you can equip new gear and level up your character. Every part of your character, from their head to their weapons are fully interchangable to allow for competely original and customizable character designs, each of which build on the character's strength to make them more powerful than ever! Try out the 1,000,000,000,000,000+ possible character options.
