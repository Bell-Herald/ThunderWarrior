How To Play

Find Out How to Play Thunder Warrior: Genesis


Creating an Account


The first step to playing is to create an account. You can create an account by clicking on the sign up button on the bar at the top of the screen. After doing so, you will be taken to a form where you create a username, email, and a password. It is important to make sure you use a secure password and your actual email adress. Next, you will have to confirm your email. You should be sent a confirmation code imediately or within the next few minutes. Enter this code into the confirmation tab. We recommend copying and pasting. After this, you will have a fully functioning account!

Navigating the Webpage


There are many aspects to the webpage, so ut may at first seem intimidating to some. On the far right of the bar is a three line icon that turns to an x and shows a drop down menu when you hover over it. This is the main menu. Within the main menu, you have access to the Home Screen, About Page, News, Updates, Contact Information, Credits, and the How to Play Page. An easy way to return to the home menu is by clicking on the Thunder Warrior: Genesis title or icon on the left of the top bar. Once you have signed in, the 'sign in / sign up' options will disapear from the top bar. They will be replaced by your username or email and a profile icon. By hovering over this name or icon, you can access ways to customize your own account such as Preferences, Information, Deletion, Confirmation, Invalidation, and Signing Out as well as a way to send us feedback or leave a review. See more about account customization here.

Joining the Game


Once you have created an account, a button saying Start Game will appear on the center of the top bar. Click this to join the game. IF it your first time playng, you will start off by choosing a class. Learn about choosing a class here. You will then be sent to the game's Homescreen. Learn about how to navigate it here and the information it displays here.

Entering the Arena


From the game's Homescreen, you will be shown at button saying Enter The Arena at the bottom of your screen. You will then be shown a list of diferent game modes to choose from, which you can learn about here. Once you have clicked on a game, you will have to wait a very short time while other players join the lobby and the 3D models of everyone's custom Thunder Warriors are built. Your Thunder Warrior will then be sent into the game and be ready for you to control.

Controlling your Thunder Warrior


Thunder Warriors are controlled using very simple keys to make the game easy for anyone to pick up. Use the arrow keys or WASD to move. If you are using a ranged weapon, aim with your mouse and click or press the space bar to fire in that direction. But be warnred, the more you shoot, the more heat your weapon gainsuntil it eventually overheats. Take a break after a time to let it cool more efficiently. By pressing F, you can switch between your melee and ranged weapon. Melee weapons also fire in the diretion you aim, but cost Stamina. Hold shift to block with a melee weapon. Learn all about Melee weapons here. Come here if you want to learn how to pick the best equipment for each match.
